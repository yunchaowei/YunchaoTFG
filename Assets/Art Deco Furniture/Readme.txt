"Art Deco Furniture" is a part of Art Deco Interior package.

https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/2d/environments/art-deco-interior-199924

It contains:
pieces of furniture (sofa, banquet and coffee table);
decorative elements (pillow, some dishes);
tilable floor texture.

In the full version you will find more stylized furniture, modular room parts, URP and HDRP support and a demoscene.
